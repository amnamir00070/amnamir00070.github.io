Construction Site Surveillance System

Zavirite is an Ai based construction site surveillance system, made for the ease of access to any under-construction place. It's for all those who want to monitor any construction site, over a web-app.

ABOUT US

the best Construction Site Surveillance System at your ease.

Construction sites face safety issues as well as slow work due to negligence of workers, Security risks don't simply involve the sort of construction project and are also related to the development location, the number of individuals that have access to the construction site, and the amount of support for security among those present on the site. Preventing theft and vandalism may be a combination of three factors: people, process, and technology. There are several technical resources out there to raise security. But, if there's no support for this among contractors and subcontractors, then the use of security technology can not be established during a process, so the protection measures have little effect. Zavirite is answer to this problem. It is a remote monitoring system for construction sites, made with latest Ai technology.
